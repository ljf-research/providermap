# NSW Legal Services Map

An interactive map showing legal assistance providers across New South Wales, Australia.

## Features

- **Service Types**: Aboriginal Legal Services (ALS), Family Violence Prevention Legal Services (FVPLS), Community Legal Centres (CLC), Legal Aid NSW offices, and Regional Outreach Community Programs (ROCP)
- **Interactive Clustering**: Markers cluster at lower zoom levels and expand as you zoom in
- **Search**: Search by service name, suburb, or postcode
- **Filters**: Toggle service types on and off
- **CLSD Boundaries**: View Community Legal Services District boundaries

## About

This map was created to help legal service providers across NSW understand legal assistance services across the state. Data includes service locations and types.

## Technology

Built with:
- R and the `leaflet` package
- OpenStreetMap / CartoDB tiles
- Leaflet.markercluster for marker clustering
